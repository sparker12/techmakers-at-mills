About Me

I am a Computer Science major at Mills College expecting to graduate in December 2018. I'm passionate about finding ways to incorporate Social Justice and Equity into even the smallest details of technology.
So far I have been working with different organizations and talking to various people about the different ways they make their practices and organizations inclusive. Whenever I attend an important conference such as Lesbians Who Tech, I keep the future generation of black and brown girls in technology in mind. I think about what would I have wanted to see in high-school and college as I transition into industry.
One of my aspirations is to be a role model and an inspiration to the young black and brown girls who will come after me.
It's important to have diversity in any company but even more so in the tech industry. Technology impacts so many lives so it doesn't make sense to limit the amount of people working behind the scenes to make life easier.
